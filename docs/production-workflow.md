# Production Workflow & Google Drive Standards
**Lumina Stream Studios | Production Operations**
**Version 2.4 | Effective March 2025**

---

## Overview

All scripted and unscripted production assets live in Google Drive. This document defines folder structure, naming conventions, access tiers, and handoff protocols for active productions.

---

## Google Drive Folder Hierarchy

Every production is allocated a root folder upon greenlight, provisioned by the Production Operations team:

```
/Productions/
  └── [YEAR]-[SHOW-CODE]-[TITLE]/
        ├── 00_ADMIN/
        │     ├── Greenlight Memo
        │     ├── Budget Tracking (locked, Finance only)
        │     └── Crew Contact Sheet
        ├── 01_DEVELOPMENT/
        │     ├── Treatment
        │     ├── Pitch Deck
        │     └── Market Research
        ├── 02_SCRIPTS/
        │     ├── Drafts/
        │     ├── Locked/
        │     └── Table Reads/
        ├── 03_SCHEDULE/
        │     ├── Stripboard/
        │     ├── One-Liner/
        │     └── Call Sheets/
        ├── 04_CASTING/
        │     └── (Legal/Business Affairs manages talent files in Box)
        ├── 05_PRODUCTION/
        │     ├── Daily Reports/
        │     ├── Camera Reports/
        │     └── Location Agreements/
        └── 06_POST/
              └── (Handoff to Post team; see Post-Production Workflow doc)
```

## Script Versioning & Locking

### Draft Scripts
- Stored in `02_SCRIPTS/Drafts/`
- Named: `[SHOW-CODE]_EP[##]_DRAFT[##]_[YYYYMMDD].pdf`
- Example: `LS024_EP03_DRAFT04_20250310.pdf`
- Access: Writers Room, Director, Producers (Editor access)

### Locked Scripts
- Moved to `02_SCRIPTS/Locked/` by the Script Supervisor after the table read
- Named: `[SHOW-CODE]_EP[##]_LOCKED_[YYYYMMDD].pdf`
- Revisions after lock use colored pages (standard WGA color order: White → Blue → Pink → Yellow)
- Access: Full cast & crew (Viewer access)

### Script Amendments
- Any revision post-lock must be logged in the **Script Change Log** (Google Sheet in `02_SCRIPTS/Locked/`)
- Notify all department heads via #production-[show-code] Slack channel

## Schedule Management

### Pre-Production
- **Master Schedule**: Maintained by the Line Producer in `03_SCHEDULE/`
- **Stripboard**: Movie Magic Scheduling file exported as PDF weekly; PDF stored in Drive
- **One-Liner**: Episode-level schedule exported from Movie Magic; updated daily during prep

### Production (Shoot Days)
- **Daily Call Sheet**: Generated by 1st AD, uploaded to `03_SCHEDULE/Call Sheets/` by 5pm the day prior
  - Named: `[SHOW-CODE]_DAY[###]_CALLSHEET_[YYYYMMDD].pdf`
- **Daily Production Report (DPR)**: Completed by Production Coordinator after wrap; uploaded same day
  - Named: `[SHOW-CODE]_DAY[###]_DPR_[YYYYMMDD].pdf`

## Access Control

| Role | Access Level |
|---|---|
| Studio Executives | Viewer (all productions) |
| Executive Producer | Editor (their productions only) |
| Line Producer | Editor (their production) |
| Department Heads | Editor (relevant folders) |
| Cast & Crew | Viewer (locked scripts, call sheets) |
| External Partners | Viewer (specific shared folders only) |

**Important**: Never share Drive links externally using "Anyone with the link." Use named sharing or restricted domain sharing (`@luminastreamstudios.com` only). For international co-production partners, create a dedicated shared drive — see the International Co-Production Guidelines.

## International Co-Productions

Google Drive is Lumina's system of record for production documents, but international co-production partners may have their own DMS. Key rules:
- Master scripts are always maintained in Lumina's Drive
- Co-producers receive Viewer access to locked scripts and final schedules only
- Joint documents (approved co-production agreements, co-financing schedules) live in a **shared drive** accessible to both parties
- All shared drives must be approved by Legal & Business Affairs before provisioning

## Handoffs

### Development → Production
Triggered by greenlight memo:
- Production Operations creates the Drive folder structure
- Line Producer completes the Crew Contact Sheet
- Writers Room folder migrated from Development

### Production → Post
At picture wrap:
- Production Coordinator compiles Final DPR and uploads to `05_PRODUCTION/`
- All locked scripts and final schedule moved to read-only (Line Producer removes edit access)
- Post-Production Supervisor provisioned with Editor access to `06_POST/`
- Jira project for post created (see Post-Production & VFX Workflow doc)

## Common Issues & FAQs

**Q: I can't find the call sheet for tomorrow.**
A: Check `03_SCHEDULE/Call Sheets/` in your show's Drive folder. If it's not posted by 6pm, contact the 1st AD or Production Coordinator via Slack (#production-[show-code]).

**Q: Who do I contact to get access to a Drive folder?**
A: Contact your department head or the Production Coordinator for your show.

**Q: Can I download scripts to my personal device?**
A: No. Scripts are confidential. Access via Google Drive only. Do not print scripts without approval from the Script Supervisor.

**Q: The budget tracking spreadsheet is locked. How do I submit a budget request?**
A: Submit a budget request via the Finance Request Form (linked in `00_ADMIN/`). Budget files are Finance-only.
