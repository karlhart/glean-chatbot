# Employee Onboarding Guide
**Lumina Stream Studios | People & Culture**
**Version 3.1 | Effective January 2025**

---

## Welcome to Lumina Stream Studios

Lumina Stream Studios is a global indie-major production house and streaming platform. We specialize in high-end docuseries and international co-productions across 14 countries. This guide covers everything you need to know in your first 30 days.

---

## Your First Week Checklist

### Day 1
- Collect your access badge from Reception (Floor 1, LA HQ or local office)
- Meet your onboarding buddy (assigned via email before your start date)
- Complete I-9 / right-to-work documentation with HR
- Set up your Lumina corporate laptop (IT will contact you with setup instructions)
- Activate your corporate Google Workspace account (firstname.lastname@luminastreamstudios.com)

### Days 2–5
- Complete mandatory compliance training modules in Workday (deadline: end of Week 1)
  - Code of Conduct
  - Anti-Harassment & Discrimination Policy
  - Data Privacy & Confidentiality (GDPR + CCPA)
  - Information Security Awareness
- Attend the weekly All-Hands (Thursdays, 10am PT / 6pm GMT) — link in Google Calendar
- Set up Slack: request access to your team channels from your manager
- Request Box access for your department via IT Help Desk ticket

## Benefits Overview

### Health & Wellness
- Medical, dental, and vision coverage effective Day 1 of employment
- Enroll via Workday within your first 30 days (missing this window requires a qualifying life event)
- Mental Health: 12 free sessions per year via Headspace Care (formerly Ginger)

### Time Off
- **PTO**: 20 days per year (accrued monthly), unlimited for VP+ roles
- **Sick Leave**: 10 days per year (separate from PTO)
- **Parental Leave**: 16 weeks fully paid (primary caregiver), 6 weeks (secondary caregiver)
- **Bereavement**: 5 days for immediate family

### Retirement
- 401(k) with 4% company match, vesting after 1 year
- Enroll via Fidelity NetBenefits (link in your welcome email)

### Other Perks
- $500/year home office stipend (approved via Expensify)
- Monthly streaming credit ($25 Lumina+ subscription)
- Annual learning budget: $1,500 (professional development, conferences, courses)

## Remote & Hybrid Work Policy

Lumina operates on a **hybrid-flexible** model:
- Employees within 50 miles of an office are expected in-office **2 days per week minimum**
- Production and post-production roles may have different schedules due to on-set or facility requirements
- Fully remote roles are designated at time of hire and require VP approval for future changes

---

## Code of Conduct Highlights

- Treat all colleagues, talent, and partners with respect
- Protect confidential information (scripts, unreleased content, talent agreements)
- Report concerns via Ethics Hotline: 1-800-LUM-ETIX or ethics@luminastreamstudios.com
- No personal use of company systems to access, store, or distribute Lumina content

---

## Expense Reimbursement

- Submit expenses via Expensify within **30 days** of the expense date
- Meals: up to $75/person for client entertainment (requires attendee list)
- Travel: book via Concur; economy class for flights under 6 hours, business class for 6+ hours
- Receipts required for any expense over $25
